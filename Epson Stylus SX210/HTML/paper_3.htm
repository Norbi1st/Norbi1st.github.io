Loading Envelopes

Follow these steps to load envelopes:
Note:
The illustrations in this section show the SX410/TX410 Series.
Open the paper support, slide out the extension, and then flip the feeder guard forward.
Lower the output tray, slide out the extension, and then raise the stopper.
Pinch and slide the edge guide.
Load envelopes short edge first with the flap facing down and to the left, and place them behind the tabs.
Slide the edge guide up to the left edge of the envelopes, but not too tightly.
Flip the feeder guard back.
Note:
The thickness of envelopes and their ability to fold vary widely. If the total thickness of the stack of envelopes exceeds 10 mm, press down on the envelopes to flatten them before loading. If print quality declines when a stack of envelopes is loaded, load one envelope at a time.
You can load up to 10 envelopes at a time in the sheet feeder.
Do not use envelopes that are curled or folded.
Flatten the envelope flaps before loading.
Flatten the leading edge of the envelope before loading.
Avoid using envelopes that are too thin, as they may curl during printing.
